Sport Class for Middle Childhood

Make your child healthy and strong by exercising regularly. This class will give your child the opportunity to make friends, practice social skills, and build confidence while playing soccer, baseball, basketball, hockey, karate, swimming, skating, and many other sports! it's a community of students who come together to share what they're learning, ask questions, participate in challenges, and get feedback on their performance
