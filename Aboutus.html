OUR GOALS

Provide high-quality education and childcare in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.

Develop well rounded, confident, and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

Provide a meaningful child-centered, play based curriculum that builds life skills, independence, confidence, and resilience to support each child in achieving their potential.


Vision

Our vision is for each child to develop a
curiosity of learning,discover their interests
and grow in their love of learning.We also
desire to have strong families through parent support/fellowship
and skills training.



Mission

Our mission is to create a better world where parents,
families and communities are empowered to create safe,
caring,nurturing environments throughout the lifespan.
We do this through creating, using and sharing scientific
knowledge gained from research on families and parenting.
