Art Class for Preschoolers

In this ongoing art class, we will create new artwork every week where students will explore art-making techniques and tools while learning how to apply elements of art in their own creative artwork inspired by artists around the world. In this comprehensive visual arts course, we will cover a variety of elements and principles of art, multicultural art, painting, drawing, 2D and 3D art. Have fun kids!!
